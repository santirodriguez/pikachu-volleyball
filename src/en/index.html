It is maintained with Linux AppImage support and ongoing quality improvements while keeping the web version working.

Controls:

If you have a Bluetooth keyboard, you can play also on your touch/mobile devices.

Tips:

1) In 1-player mode ("1人でぴかちゅ~"), press Z to play as Player 1 or Enter to play as Player 2.

2) Player 1 has an extra down input on the V key. It behaves differently from F, so use the key that fits your timing.

3) Press Esc to hide or show the menu bar.

4) Practice mode is available at "Options → Practice mode → on".

5) For offline play in a browser, install the web app from your browser menu or add this page to your home screen.

Press Power Hit for a quick rematch

The winning score can not be set to the pts, since the score is already reached.

There's no point adjusting the winning score in practice mode.
You can turn off practice mode at "Options → Practice mode → off".
